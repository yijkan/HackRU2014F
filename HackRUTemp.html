Instruction

Healing Humanity is a game focused on promoted world equality.
You must keep the human body, or the world, from dying from ailments.

  1. Click Play.
  2. If you have enough heals, click injured areas of the body.
  3. Heals will regenerate over time.
  4. Prevent any part from reaching critical level and dying.
  5. Save Humanity!
